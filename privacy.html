Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03-Feb-2025

At Dropstore, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to protect your data when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide you with a seamless shopping experience. This includes:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up or make a purchase, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment details.
  • Account Information: To facilitate your shopping, we may store your preferences, order history, and saved items.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you use our website and app, including pages visited, clicks, and interactions. This helps us improve our services and user experience.
  • Device and Log Information: We may collect information about the device you're using, IP address, browser type, and operating system for analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Processing Orders: To complete your purchases, deliver your products, and manage your orders.
  • Personalizing Experience: To provide recommendations, promotions, and a tailored shopping experience.
  • Improving Our Services: To analyze and improve the functionality and user experience of our website and app.
  • Marketing and Communications: To send you promotional emails, app notifications, or special offers if you opt-in to receive them.
  • Compliance and Security: To ensure compliance with applicable laws and protect your account and personal data from fraud or unauthorized access.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes. However, we may share your information with the following:

  • Service Providers: We may use trusted third-party vendors to assist in processing orders, payments, and providing services like shipping.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights and interests, such as in response to a legal request or investigation.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain conditions.

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please contact us at help.dropstore@gmail.com.
